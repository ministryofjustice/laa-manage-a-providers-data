Manage a Provider's Data

Use this service to manage LAA accounts for legal aid providers, including legal services providers, chambers, advocates and barristers.

You will be able to view and amend details about:

  • firms
  • offices
  • contact details
  • bank accounts
  • contracts
  • schedules

This service replaces the CWA web interface.
